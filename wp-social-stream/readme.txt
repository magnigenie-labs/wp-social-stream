== Description ==

WP Social Stream is a simple, light weight plugin which allows you to show a stream of your online activities on your site. Now combine all of your social network interactions into one single network stream and display on your site.

This plugin has easy to use configuration, which allows you to add your different social network accounts , customize look and feel of the the plugin to match your site design.

= Features: =

* Fully responsive front end.
* Easy to use admin interface.
* Ability to modify look and feel of the plugin.
* Display your online activity from 46 different sites.
* Uses shortcode to display your activities on your site.
* Adds ability to put shortcodes on widgets too.

= Important Notes =
This plugin is based on  [jQuery Lifestream](https://github.com/christianv/jquery-lifestream).

== Installation ==

1. Go to your admin area and select Plugins -> Add new from the menu.
2. Search for "WP Social Stream".
3. Click install.
4. Click activate.
5. Go to Settings > WP Social Stream and start playing with the configurations.
